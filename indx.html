TAJ MAHAL

Agara, India

The Taj Mahal is the seventh wonder of the world, which is located in Agra, India. The Taj
Mahal is one of the most famous structures in the world built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan
in memory of his third wife, Mumtaz. It is one of the most beautiful building in the world and
is a symbol of love.
